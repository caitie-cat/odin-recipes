Spaghetti

Description

This delicous spaghetti recipe is one of my favorite recipes to make (and one of the only ones I can make because I can't cook). It's one of the few dinners that my entire family will eat.

Ingredients

  • 1 jar of premade spaghetti sauce
  • 1 box of spaghetti noodles
  • fresh basil for garnish

Steps

  1. Put spaghetti in medium saucepan on medium-high until boiling.
  2. While sauce is cooking, fill dutch oven with water. Bring to a boil.
  3. Once the water is boiling, cook noodles for 5-7 minutes.
  4. By now, the sauce should be done. Put the sauce down to a simmer.
  5. After noodles are done cooking, strain in a strainer.
  6. Dish it all up and it's ready to serve! Garnish with fresh basil if you desire.
