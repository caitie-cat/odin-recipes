Fajitas

Description

Fajitas are my go-to chicken dish, especially because it doesn't take much time to prepare at all. Add a bit of greek yogurt or sour cream on top of them and they're complete!

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 package of premade fajita seasoning
  • a pack of your favorite tortillas

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. While waiting for oven to preheat, chop up onion and green pepper in thin slices.
  3. Cut chicken into sizeable strips.
  4. Mix oil and fajita seasoning in a large bowl. Add chicken and veggies, coating evenly with the seasoning.
  5. Prepare baking sheet with foil and nonstick spray. Add coated chicken and veggies on pan in an even layer.
  6. Cook fajitas for 15-20 minutes. When finished, put filling into tortillas and enjoy!
